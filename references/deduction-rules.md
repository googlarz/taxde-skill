## 1. Werbungskosten

### 1.1 Homeoffice Pauschale
- **Name**: Homeoffice Tagespauschale (daily flat rate)
- **2024**: €6/day, max 210 days = max **€1,260/year**
- **2023**: €6/day — rate increased from €5 in 2023 (Jahressteuergesetz 2022)
- **Legal basis**: §4 Abs. 5 Nr. 6b EStG; §9 Abs. 5 S. 1 EStG
- **Conditions**:
  - Must actually work from home that day (not just log in briefly)
  - Can combine with Pendlerpauschale on same day only if different workplaces
  - Applies whether you have dedicated room or share the kitchen table
- **Documentation**: Day log (calendar entries, email timestamps sufficient)
- **Common mistakes**: Claiming days when you also claimed the full Arbeitszimmer deduction for an overlapping period
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive — clear rule, very low audit risk
- **Alternative**: Arbeitszimmer (if dedicated room) — see §1.2

### 1.2 Arbeitszimmer (Dedicated Home Office)
- **2024**: Actual costs (rent/mortgage share, electricity, internet, furniture) — no cap if "Mittelpunkt der gesamten beruflichen Tätigkeit"
- **Pauschale alternative**: €1,260/year flat if not the primary workplace
- **Legal basis**: §4 Abs. 5 Nr. 6b EStG (post-JStG 2022 reform)
- **Conditions**:
  - Dedicated room used almost exclusively (>90%) for professional purposes
  - No private use (no guest bed, no gaming setup)
  - Proportional share = room m² / total apartment m²
- **Documentation**: Floor plan, rental contract, utility bills
- **Finanzamt scrutiny**: HIGH — most claimed Arbeitszimmer are challenged
- **Confidence**: 🔶 Debatable — genuine gray area; document extensively

### 1.3 Pendlerpauschale (Commuting)
- **Rate**: €0.30/km for first 20 km; **€0.38/km** for km 21+
- **Calculation**: km (one way) × working days per year × rate
- **Cap**: None for private car; public transport: actual costs if higher
- **Legal basis**: §9 Abs. 1 Nr. 4 EStG
- **Conditions**: Only the shorter route (kürzeste Straßenverbindung) unless longer route demonstrably faster
- **Documentation**: Nothing required if under 20 km; for longer routes, consider map screenshot
- **Common mistakes**: Counting both ways (it's one-way), including holiday days
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive

### 1.4 Arbeitsmittel (Work Equipment)
- **Items**: Laptop, monitor, desk, chair, headset, books, tools, work clothing (non-civilian)
- **Under €800 net** (€952 gross incl. 19% VAT): fully deductible in year of purchase
- **Over €800 net**: must depreciate over useful life (AFA table):
  - Laptop/computer: 3 years
  - Monitor: 3 years
  - Desk/chair: 13 years
  - Smartphone: 3 years
- **Business use %**: Must estimate if mixed-use (typical: 60-80% for laptop, 100% for dedicated work gear)
- **Legal basis**: §9 Abs. 1 Nr. 6 EStG
- **Soft scrutiny threshold**: ~€2,000 total — be prepared to justify above this
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive for under €800; ⚠️ Likely for high amounts

### 1.5 Fortbildungskosten (Education & Training)
- **Items**: Courses, conferences, professional exams, language classes (if job-relevant), Umschulung
- **Amount**: Actual costs — no cap
- **Legal basis**: §9 Abs. 1 Nr. 6 EStG
- **Conditions**: Job-related; not initial education (that's Sonderausgaben)
- **Documentation**: Invoice + brief note on job relevance
- **Confidence**: ✅ for clearly job-related; ⚠️ for borderline courses

### 1.6 Internet & Telefon
- **Deductible**: ~20% of internet/phone bill for professional use (conservative)
- Up to €20/month (€240/year) typically accepted without documentation
- Higher amounts require evidence of professional use
- **Legal basis**: §9 Abs. 1 Nr. 6 EStG
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive for 20%; ⚠️ Likely above

### 1.7 Gewerkschaftsbeiträge (Union Dues)
- **Amount**: 100% of annual dues
- **Legal basis**: §9 Abs. 1 Nr. 3d EStG
- **Documentation**: Annual statement from union
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive

### 1.8 Doppelte Haushaltsführung (Double Household)
- **Amount**: Up to €1,000/month accommodation costs + travel home + meals first 3 months
- **Conditions**: Primary residence elsewhere (must be genuine Erstwohnsitz), commute >50 km OR >1h each way
- **Legal basis**: §9 Abs. 1 Nr. 5 EStG
- **Confidence**: ⚠️ Likely — but requires solid documentation of primary residence

### 1.9 Bewerbungskosten (Job Application Costs)
- **Amount**: Actual costs (postage, photos, copies, travel to interviews)
- **Typical**: €8.50/application flat accepted by many Finanzämter
- **Confidence**: ⚠️ Likely

## 2. Sonderausgaben

### 2.1 Kranken- & Pflegeversicherung
- **Deductible**: Employee share of GKV contributions + Pflegeversicherung
- **GKV**: Employee pays 7.3% + half of Zusatzbeitrag; employer share is NOT deductible
- **2024 typical total employee KV**: ~9% of gross (GKV 7.3% + Pflege 1.7% + Zusatzbeitrag ~0.9%)
- **PKV**: Actual Basisabsicherung premiums deductible; supplemental riders not
- **Legal basis**: §10 Abs. 1 Nr. 3 EStG
- **Documentation**: Beitragsbescheinigung from Krankenkasse (mandatory since 2010)
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive

### 2.2 Riester-Rente
- **Max Sonderausgaben deduction**: €2,100/year (contributions + state Zulage)
- **Zulage**: €175 Grundzulage + €185/child (born before 2008) or €300/child (born 2008+)
- **Günstigerprüfung**: Finanzamt automatically checks if §10a deduction beats Zulage
- **Legal basis**: §10a EStG, §79–99 EStG
- **Documentation**: Annual Riester Bescheinigung from provider
- **Who benefits most**: Lower-middle income earners with children (Zulage is relatively more valuable)
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive

### 2.3 Rürup (Basisrente)
- **Max deductible 2024**: Up to the BBG RV × 23.8% = ~€27,565 (West) — effectively all of it
- **2024**: 100% deductible (reached 100% in 2023)
- **Legal basis**: §10 Abs. 1 Nr. 2b EStG
- **Who benefits most**: Self-employed, high earners without other Altersvorsorge
- **Note**: Cannot be cancelled, only suspended — illiquid until age 62
- **Documentation**: Annual provider statement
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive

### 2.4 Betriebliche Altersvorsorge (bAV)
- **Tax-free via salary sacrifice**:
  - Up to 4% BBG RV = **€3,624** (2024) — tax AND social contribution free
  - Additional up to 4% BBG RV = additional **€1,800** — tax free only
- **Legal basis**: §3 Nr. 63 EStG
- **Documentation**: Employer payslips showing bAV deduction; annual statement
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive

### 2.5 Kinderbetreuungskosten
- **2024**: 2/3 of costs, up to €4,000 per child per year
- **2025+**: 80% of costs, up to €4,800 per child per year
- **Age**: Child under 14 (or disabled child of any age)
- **Legal basis**: §10 Abs. 1 Nr. 5 EStG
- **Payment method**: Bank transfer required (no cash)
- **Documentation**: Invoice + proof of payment
- **Common mistakes**: Including food, clothing, activities in the amount (only care/supervision)
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive

### 2.6 Kirchensteuer
- **Rate**: 9% of income tax in most states; 8% in Bayern and Baden-Württemberg
- **Itself deductible**: Yes — the Kirchensteuer you paid is deductible as Sonderausgaben
- **Legal basis**: §10 Abs. 1 Nr. 4 EStG
- **Documentation**: Automatically on Lohnsteuerbescheinigung (field 24/25)
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive

### 2.7 Spenden (Donations)
- **Up to €300**: Simplified proof (Kontoauszug/Überweisungsbeleg)
- **Over €300**: Zuwendungsbestätigung from the organization required
- **Annual cap**: 20% of total income (excess carries forward 1 year)
- **Organisation check**: Must be gemeinnützig (§52 AO) — check Zuwendungsempfänger register
- **Legal basis**: §10b EStG
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive if documentation correct

### 2.8 Unterhalt (Realsplitting)
- **Amount**: Up to €13,805 deductible if ex-spouse agrees (Anlage U)
- **Trade-off**: Ex-spouse must declare as taxable income → usually compensated by payer
- **Legal basis**: §10 Abs. 1 Nr. 5 EStG
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive but requires cooperation

## 4. Family Deductions

### 4.1 Kindergeld vs. Kinderfreibetrag (Günstigerprüfung)
- **Kindergeld**: 2024 = €250/month, 2025 = €255/month, 2026 = €259/month per child
- **Kinderfreibetrag total per child (couple)**:
  - 2024: (€3,306 × 2) + (€1,464 × 2) = **€9,540**
  - 2025: (€3,336 × 2) + (€1,464 × 2) = **€9,600**
  - 2026: (€3,414 × 2) + (€1,464 × 2) = **€9,756**
- **Günstigerprüfung**: Finanzamt automatically checks which is more favorable — you cannot choose
- At **gross ~€40,000** (single), KFB typically doesn't beat Kindergeld
- At **gross ~€70,000+** (single), KFB starts to win
- **Legal basis**: §31, §32 VI EStG
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive (Finanzamt calculates)

### 4.2 Entlastungsbetrag Alleinerziehende
- **Amount**: €4,260 + €240 per additional child after the first
- **Conditions**: Not in Haushaltsgemeinschaft with another adult; child registered at your address
- **Legal basis**: §24b EStG; Steuerklasse II
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive

## 5. Haushaltsnahe Aufwendungen §35a

### 5.1 Handwerkerleistungen
- **Credit**: 20% of **labor costs only** (not materials)
- **Max labor for credit**: €6,000/year → max **credit €1,200/year**
- **Conditions**:
  - Must be in your principal or secondary residence
  - Payment by bank transfer (cash = 0€ credit)
  - Invoice must separately state labor vs. materials
- **Examples**: Plumber, electrician, painter, cleaner, garden work, chimney sweep
- **NOT included**: New builds, publicly subsidized work, maintenance by landlord
- **Legal basis**: §35a Abs. 3 EStG
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive if documented correctly

### 5.2 Haushaltsnahe Dienstleistungen
- **Credit**: 20% of costs
- **Max**: €20,000/year → max **credit €4,000/year**
- **Examples**: Cleaning service, au pair, elderly care at home, garden maintenance
- **Same rules**: Bank transfer required; no cash
- **Legal basis**: §35a Abs. 2 EStG
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive

## 6. Kapitalerträge

### 6.1 Sparer-Pauschbetrag
- **2024**: €1,000 single / €2,000 married couple
- **Setup**: Must file Freistellungsauftrag at each bank (max total = €1,000/€2,000 across all banks)
- **Legal basis**: §20 Abs. 9 EStG
- **Common miss**: People with multiple banks who haven't split the Freistellungsauftrag

### 6.2 Günstigerprüfung (Capital Income)
- If your marginal income tax rate < 25% (Abgeltungsteuer), you can apply individual rate
- Applies to: retirees, low earners, students with capital income
- **File**: Anlage KAP, check Günstigerprüfung box
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive

### 6.3 Verlustverrechnungstöpfe
- **Types**: Aktien-Verlustverrechnungstopf (only offset with stock gains) and general
- **Strategy**: Harvest losses before year-end to offset gains in same year
- Cannot carry losses to next year within same year — use or lose

### 6.4 Vorabpauschale
- Annual "phantom gain" on accumulating ETFs — taxed even if not sold
- Published by BMF in January each year (Basiszins × 0.7 × fund value × 30% reduction for equity funds)
- 2024 Basiszins: verify with BMF publication in January 2025
- **Confidence**: ✅ Definitive once Basiszins published

## Annual Verification Checklist

Before filing each year, verify these have not changed:
- [ ] Grundfreibetrag (usually increases with inflation)
- [ ] Homeoffice Tagespauschale rate and max days
- [ ] Pendlerpauschale rates
- [ ] BBG RV (affects Rürup max and bAV limits)
- [ ] Rürup deductible percentage (reached 100% in 2023)
- [ ] Riester Zulage amounts
- [ ] Kinderbetreuungskosten limits
- [ ] Kindergeld amounts
- [ ] Kinderfreibetrag amounts
- [ ] Vorabpauschale Basiszins (January BMF publication)
- [ ] Kleinunternehmergrenze (effective €25,000 / €100,000 from 2025 onward)
